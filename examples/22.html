算法讲解 Algorithm Explanation
步骤 1 / Step 1: 冒泡排序是一种简单的排序算法，它重复地遍历要排序的列表，比较相邻的元素，如果它们的顺序错误就交换它们。

Bubble Sort is a simple sorting algorithm that repeatedly steps through the list, compares adjacent elements and swaps them if they are in the wrong order.
步骤 2 / Step 2: 遍历列表的工作会重复进行，直到列表完全排序。每次遍历都会将最大的元素"冒泡"到其正确位置。

The pass through the list is repeated until the list is sorted. Each pass bubbles the largest element to its correct position.
步骤 3 / Step 3: 该算法之所以称为"冒泡排序"，是因为较小的元素会像气泡一样逐渐"浮"到列表的顶端。

The algorithm gets its name because smaller elements "bubble" to the top of the list gradually.
步骤 4 / Step 4: 冒泡排序的时间复杂度在最坏和平均情况下为O(n²)，在最好情况下为O(n)。

Bubble Sort has a time complexity of O(n²) in worst and average cases, and O(n) in the best case.
